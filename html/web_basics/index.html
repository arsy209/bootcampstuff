Welcome to the Cricket lovers page!!

Some great information about Cricket that will fascinate you:

  1. It first originated in England and soon spread to other countries.
  2. It is known as a bat and ball game which is played between two teams that have 11 players on a cricket field
  3. One team would have 11 players on the field including a wicket keeper on one end of a stump and a bowlering bowling on the other end.

Cricket equipment:

  • Cricket bat
  • cricket ball (usually red or white) and stumps.
  • Wicket keeping gloves and pads.
  • Helmet, Batting gloves, pads, arm, thigh and leg pads.
  • Team uniform, shoes, cup
